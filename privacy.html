Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Summary

Hebrew Dates helps you calculate Hebrew calendar dates and optionally sync them to a dedicated Google Calendar. The app is designed to avoid collecting personal information on a server.

Information stored in your browser

If you use the app without Google, your events are stored in your browser using localStorage. This can include names, event labels, Hebrew dates, and event types that you enter. This data stays on your device unless you export an ICS file or connect Google Calendar.

Google Calendar access

If you connect Google Calendar, the app asks permission to create and manage calendars created by this app, list your Google calendars to find app-created calendars, and share an app-created calendar when you choose to share it.

Google access tokens are used in your browser to call Google Calendar APIs. The app does not run a backend server that stores your Google access token, calendar contents, names, dates, calendar IDs, or email addresses.

Calendar sharing

If you choose to share a calendar, the email address you type is sent directly from your browser to Google Calendar so Google can add that person to the calendar access list. Hebrew Dates does not store that email address on a server.

Analytics

The app may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage, such as app opens, event creation, ICS downloads, sync completion, and sharing role. Analytics events do not include names, Hebrew dates, Gregorian dates, email addresses, calendar IDs, or event contents.

Third-party services

The app may use Google Identity Services, Google Calendar API, Google Analytics, Vercel hosting, and Hebcal calendar libraries delivered through an ESM CDN. These services may process technical information such as IP address, browser metadata, and request logs according to their own policies.

Google API Limited Use

Use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data deletion

You can delete local browser data by removing events in the app or clearing your browser storage for this site. You can revoke Google access from your Google Account security settings. You can delete synced calendars and events from Google Calendar.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ohadc7@gmail.com.

מדיניות פרטיות בעברית

האפליקציה תאריכים עבריים מחשבת תאריכים עבריים ומאפשרת, לפי בחירתכם, לסנכרן אותם ליומן Google ייעודי. אין לאפליקציה שרת ששומר את פרטי האירועים שלכם.

במצב ללא Google, האירועים נשמרים בדפדפן שלכם בלבד. בחיבור ל-Google Calendar, האפליקציה משתמשת בהרשאות שקיבלתם כדי ליצור ולנהל יומנים שנוצרו על ידי האפליקציה, למצוא יומנים כאלה, ולשתף יומן רק כאשר אתם מבקשים זאת.

האפליקציה לא שומרת בשרת שמות, תאריכים, כתובות אימייל, מזהי יומנים, תוכן אירועים או אסימוני גישה של Google. ייתכן שנעשה שימוש ב-Google Analytics למדידת שימוש מצטברת בלבד, ללא תוכן אישי של האירועים.

שימוש והעברה של מידע שמתקבל מ-Google APIs יעמדו במדיניות Google API Services User Data Policy, כולל דרישות Limited Use.
